❄ A Special Letter from the North Pole ❄

OFFICIAL ELF CORRESPONDENCE
❄ ❄ ❄ ❄ ❄
Dear Brooks, Harper, Vivian, and Hayes,

Guess who's BACK! That's right — it's ME, Snowflake! I've been counting down the days until I could fly back to your house, and tomorrow is finally the day! Santa has given me a very special mission this year, and I couldn't be more excited to share it with you!

🎁 Big News: I'm Bringing a Baby Elf! 🎁

This year, Santa asked me to do something very important. You see, at the North Pole, we have a special Elf Training Program. Young elves need to learn how to be Santa's helpers by watching wonderful children like YOU!

So I'm bringing along a teeny tiny baby elf who will be learning from me — and from all of you! This little one has never visited a family before and is SO nervous but also SO excited. The baby elf needs YOUR help with something very special...

✎ Your Important Mission:

  • Name the Baby Elf! — This little elf doesn't have a name yet. Santa said YOUR family gets to choose it! Talk together and pick a perfect name.
  • Write the name on a piece of paper and leave it near us tomorrow night.
  • Be extra kind and cheerful — baby elves learn by watching!
  • Remember: No touching! Baby elves are even more delicate than me.

I'll be watching to make sure everyone is being kind, helpful, and full of Christmas spirit. Santa is SO proud of all four of you already, and I can't wait to report back all the wonderful things you do this season!

With candy canes and Christmas magic,

Snowflake ❄

(and one very excited baby elf!)

P.S. — See you when you wake up on December 1st! Start thinking of names!
