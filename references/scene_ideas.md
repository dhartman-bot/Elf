# 25 Days of Elf on the Shelf Scene Ideas

For Snowflake (and baby elf!) with Brooks, Harper, Vivian, and Hayes

---

## Week 1: Arrival & Getting Settled (Dec 1-7)

### Day 1 (Dec 1) - ARRIVAL DAY
**Scene:** Grand Arrival with Baby Elf
- **Setup:** Snowflake arrives with baby elf in a tiny basket/box "carrier", arrival letter nearby, small bag of "North Pole snow" (cotton balls or fake snow)
- **Props:** Baby elf, small basket, cotton balls, arrival letter, tiny blanket
- **Note:** Include naming instructions for baby elf
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 2 (Dec 2) - Name Celebration
**Scene:** Baby Elf's Name Announcement
- **Setup:** Elves surrounded by tiny letter tiles/Scrabble pieces spelling out chosen name, mini celebration banner
- **Props:** Letter tiles, small ribbon/banner, confetti
- **Note:** Thank you note for naming baby elf
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 3 (Dec 3) - Kitchen Mischief
**Scene:** Marshmallow Bubble Bath
- **Setup:** Elves in a bowl or mug filled with mini marshmallows, tiny towel nearby
- **Props:** Bowl/mug, mini marshmallows, small fabric scrap for towel
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 4 (Dec 4) - Movie Night
**Scene:** Watching Christmas Movies
- **Setup:** Elves sitting in front of TV with tiny popcorn bowl (made from bottle cap), blanket
- **Props:** Bottle cap, popped popcorn kernels, small fabric
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 5 (Dec 5) - Teaching Moment
**Scene:** Snowflake Teaching Baby Elf
- **Setup:** Mini "classroom" with elves at small table, tiny chalkboard with "How to Watch for Santa" written on it
- **Props:** Small paper/cardboard chalkboard, tiny desk (matchbox), chalk drawing
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 6 (Dec 6) - Breakfast Surprise
**Scene:** Elves Made Breakfast
- **Setup:** Elves with a plate of donuts/treats and a note saying "We made breakfast!"
- **Props:** Donuts or treats, small note
- **Note:** Praise for good behavior yesterday
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 7 (Dec 7) - Craft Time
**Scene:** Making Paper Snowflakes
- **Setup:** Elves surrounded by tiny scissors, paper scraps, and mini paper snowflakes
- **Props:** Small scissors, paper scraps, pre-cut tiny snowflakes
- **Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Week 2: Adventures & Mischief (Dec 8-14)

### Day 8 (Dec 8) - Zip Line Adventure
**Scene:** Zip Line Across the Room
- **Setup:** String/ribbon stretched across room, elves hanging from it with small hangers or clips
- **Props:** String/ribbon, clips or small hangers, attach to light fixtures/shelves
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 9 (Dec 9) - Fishing Trip
**Scene:** Fishing in the Sink
- **Setup:** Elves with tiny fishing poles (stick + string) over a sink with goldfish crackers
- **Props:** Small sticks, string, goldfish crackers
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 10 (Dec 10) - Snow Angel
**Scene:** Making Snow Angels
- **Setup:** Elves lying in flour/powdered sugar on counter making "snow angels"
- **Props:** Flour or powdered sugar, counter space (easy cleanup tip: use parchment paper underneath)
- **Difficulty:** Medium (messy)

### Day 11 (Dec 11) - Game Night
**Scene:** Playing Games
- **Setup:** Elves with mini cards, dice, or board game pieces scattered around
- **Props:** Playing cards, dice, or small game pieces
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 12 (Dec 12) - Photo Booth
**Scene:** Silly Photo Booth
- **Setup:** Elves posing with family photos, maybe drew mustaches on printed photos (use dry erase on frames!)
- **Props:** Family photos, dry-erase markers, tiny props
- **Note:** "We love your family!"
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 13 (Dec 13) - Camping Trip
**Scene:** Indoor Camping
- **Setup:** Tiny tent (napkin/tissue over sticks), mini campfire (orange/red tissue paper), elves with tiny s'mores
- **Props:** Napkin, sticks, tissue paper, mini marshmallows
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 14 (Dec 14) - Gift Wrapping Station
**Scene:** Wrapping Presents
- **Setup:** Elves surrounded by ribbon, small wrapped boxes, tape dispenser
- **Props:** Small boxes, wrapping paper scraps, ribbon
- **Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Week 3: Holiday Spirit (Dec 15-21)

### Day 15 (Dec 15) - Gingerbread House
**Scene:** Building a Gingerbread House
- **Setup:** Elves with graham crackers and icing, partially "built" house
- **Props:** Graham crackers, icing, candy decorations
- **Note:** Recipe card for family to make one together
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 16 (Dec 16) - Snowball Fight
**Scene:** Epic Snowball Fight
- **Setup:** Elves hiding behind barriers (books, boxes) with mini snowballs (cotton balls, white pom poms)
- **Props:** Cotton balls/pom poms, small barriers
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 17 (Dec 17) - North Pole Mail
**Scene:** Letters to Santa Station
- **Setup:** Elves with tiny mailbox, envelopes, and note encouraging kids to write Santa
- **Props:** Small box as mailbox, mini envelopes, paper, crayons nearby
- **Note:** Special mission - write letters to Santa!
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 18 (Dec 18) - Hot Cocoa Party
**Scene:** Hot Chocolate Bar
- **Setup:** Elves with tiny mugs (thimbles or bottle caps), marshmallows, cocoa packets
- **Props:** Thimbles/bottle caps, mini marshmallows, cocoa mix
- **Note:** Suggest family hot cocoa night
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 19 (Dec 19) - Decorating Tree
**Scene:** Hanging Ornaments
- **Setup:** Elves climbing the Christmas tree, tangled in garland or hanging on ornament
- **Props:** Christmas tree access, secure positioning
- **Difficulty:** Medium (need to secure elves safely)

### Day 20 (Dec 20) - Story Time
**Scene:** Reading Christmas Stories
- **Setup:** Elves with a tiny book or sitting on a Christmas book, reading to stuffed animals
- **Props:** Small book or Christmas storybook, stuffed animals arranged as "audience"
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 21 (Dec 21) - Caroling
**Scene:** Singing Carols
- **Setup:** Elves with tiny songbooks (folded paper), lined up like a choir
- **Props:** Tiny folded paper "songbooks", arrange elves in formation
- **Note:** List of family's favorite Christmas songs
- **Difficulty:** Easy

---

## Week 4: The Final Countdown (Dec 22-25)

### Day 22 (Dec 22) - Cookie Baking
**Scene:** Baking Cookies for Santa
- **Setup:** Elves in kitchen with flour, mini cookie cutters, note about making cookies for Santa
- **Props:** Flour, small bowl, cookie cutters
- **Note:** Cookie recipe for Santa's cookies
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 23 (Dec 23) - Wrapping Party
**Scene:** Final Gift Wrapping Chaos
- **Setup:** Elves tangled in ribbon and wrapping paper, surrounded by tape and scissors
- **Props:** Wrapping paper scraps, ribbon, tape
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Day 24 (Dec 24) - THE GOODBYE
**Scene:** Goodbye Setup
- **Setup:** Elves with packed tiny suitcase, goodbye letter, small gift for each child (candy cane, small treat)
- **Props:** Tiny suitcase (matchbox), goodbye letter, small treats
- **Note:** Heartfelt goodbye letter, promise to return next year
- **Difficulty:** Medium

### Day 25 (Dec 25) - GONE!
**Scene:** Elves Have Left
- **Setup:** Empty spot where elves usually sit, with a small note or sprinkle of "magic dust" (glitter)
- **Props:** Small note, tiny bit of glitter
- **Note:** "See you next year! Love, Snowflake & [Baby Elf Name]"
- **Difficulty:** Easy

## Bonus Ideas (Swap in as needed)

**Scene:** Doctor Checkup
- **Setup:** Elf in tissue box "bed" with tiny thermometer, encourage rest and feel better
- **Props:** Tissue box, small paper thermometer
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Weekend Special
**Scene:** Movie Theater
- **Setup:** Elves set up movie with popcorn, suggest family movie night
- **Props:** Popcorn, small "tickets" made of paper
- **Difficulty:** Easy

### Behavior Reminder

- **Props:** Paper scroll, letter
- **Note:** Gentle reminder about specific behavior
- **Difficulty:** Easy

- **Setup:** Elves with tiny birthday banner, small gift

- **Difficulty:** Easy
